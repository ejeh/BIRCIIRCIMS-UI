How to apply

Here is how to register on
BICRIRMS

01

Create an Account

Begin by creating your personalized account on our platform, login in and request for certificate.

02

Fill in Your Details

Provide the necessary information to complete your application.

03

Upload Supporting Documents.

Attach your passport and Your NIN and other requested document to verify your identity and establish your connection to Benue state.

04

Get Certificate

After Submission, your application will be approved and your certificate will be sent to your email for your use, incase if you didn't get an email proceed to check your dashboard. You can print at liberty.

Why Choose us

Here is why you should choose
BICRIRMS

01

Secure and Verified Identity

Registering on BICRIRMS ensures that your identity is securely verified and stored in a state-of-the-art digital system. You’ll receive a Benue Identity Number (BIN) that acts as your unique identifier for accessing a range of services across the state.

02

Access Essential Services

With your registered identity, you can easily access a variety of government and private services, including:

  • Banking
  • Healthcare
  • Law Enforcement
  • Education
  • Tax Services
03

Emergency Support

In case of an emergency, emergency responders can quickly access your critical health information (e.g., blood type, allergies) and next-of-kin details through the system. This can save time and even lives in urgent situations.

04

Digital and Physical Identity Cards

A virtual identity card that you can access on your mobile phone, which can be used for online and offline identity verification. A physical identity card with advanced security features, making it a reliable proof of your identity across various sectors.

05

Quick and Easy Registration Process

The registration process is straightforward and includes features like "Know Your Neighbor" for added security. You can easily provide your family members' and neighbors' contact details to ensure transparency and accountability.

06

Indigeneship and Residency Validation

For citizens of Benue, the system enables easy validation of your indigeneship certificate by local chiefs and government authorities. Residents will be able to validate their status through community leader references or utility bills.

07

Government Services at Your Fingertips

Once registered, you’ll have easy access to a wide range of government services directly through the platform. This includes things like tax filings, updating your residency status, and applying for various public services.

08

Enhanced Security

The platform is built with enterprise-grade security, including multi-factor authentication and data encryption to protect your personal information from unauthorized access.

09

Convenience and Accessibility

Mobile App: Access and manage your digital identity anywhere, anytime, via a secure mobile app. Web Portal: Organizations and government agencies can easily verify your identity through the online portal.
